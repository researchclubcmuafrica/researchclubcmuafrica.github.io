Keynote Speaker
Opening Keynote

Mr. Samuel Rutunda

CTO, Digital Umuganda

Digital Umuganda's CTO opened the Research Showcase with an inspiring keynote, sharing industry perspectives on how cutting-edge African tech innovation is reshaping digital services across the continent. Drawing from his experience building impactful technology products for the African market, Mr. Rutunda challenged researchers to bridge the gap between academic inquiry and real-world deployment.

"The best research doesn't end in a paper — it ends in a product that changes someone's life. Africa's researchers are uniquely positioned to solve African problems. This showcase is proof of that."
Research Assistant Sessions

RA Sessions

Two exceptional researchers presented their ongoing work, showcasing the depth and breadth of graduate research at CMU Africa.

Healthcare AI

Enhanced Cardiovascular Diseases Recovery in Medically Underserved Communities via AI-Assisted Stethoscopy

Mona Aman
RA Presenter

Exploring how AI-powered diagnostic tools can extend quality cardiovascular care to communities that lack access to traditional specialist resources — combining deep learning with acoustic stethoscope signals.

Social Robotics

Culturally Sensitive Social Robotics for Africa

Yohannes Haile
RA Presenter

Designing robotic systems that honour African cultural norms, social cues, and linguistic diversity — moving beyond Western-centric robotics frameworks to build machines that feel familiar and trustworthy to African users.
