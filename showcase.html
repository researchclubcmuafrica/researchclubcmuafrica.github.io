Opening Keynote
Keynote Speaker

Mr. Samuel Rutunda

CTO, Digital Umuganda  ·  Kigali, Rwanda

Mr. Rutunda opened the Research Showcase with an industry perspective on how African-led technology is reshaping digital infrastructure across the continent. Drawing from his work building civic technology products at Digital Umuganda, he challenged researchers to close the gap between academic inquiry and deployable solutions that serve real communities.

"The best research doesn't end in a paper - it ends in a product that changes someone's life. Africa's researchers are uniquely positioned to solve African problems. This showcase is proof of that."
Graduate Research

RA Sessions

Two researchers presented their ongoing work, showcasing the depth of graduate-level inquiry at CMU Africa.

  Healthcare AI

Enhanced Cardiovascular Diseases Recovery in Medically Underserved Communities via AI-Assisted Stethoscopy

Mona Aman

Exploring how AI-powered acoustic diagnostics can extend quality cardiovascular care to communities lacking specialist access - combining deep learning with stethoscope signals to enable early, remote detection.

  Social Robotics

Culturally Sensitive Social Robotics for Africa

Yohannes Haile

Designing robotic systems that honour African cultural norms, social cues, and linguistic diversity - moving beyond Western-centric frameworks to build machines that feel familiar and trustworthy to African communities.
