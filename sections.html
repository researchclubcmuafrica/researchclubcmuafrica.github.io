Renewable Energy Published

Simulation of PEM Electrolyzer Power Management with Renewable Generation

The study models a hybrid renewable-powered PEM electrolyzer to optimize hydrogen production and provide affordable, reliable energy for sub-Saharan Africa.

Advisor: Prof. Jesse Thornburg Team: Chisom Ogbogu (MSECE-AD)

Deep Learning Published

Improving SSVEP BCI Spellers With Data Augmentation and Language Models

The research improved SSVEP-based brain-computer interface (BCI) spellers by using data augmentation and language models to boost accuracy and reliability, enabling more intuitive communication even when brain signals are unclear.

Advisors: Bhiksha Ramakrishnan & Rita Singh Team: Kipngeno Koech (MSEAI)
Learn More
Energy Systems Published

Smart Grid Fault Mitigation and Cybersecurity with Wide-Area Measurement Systems: A Review

This review examines strategies for mitigating smart grid faults and enhancing cybersecurity using Wide-Area Measurement Systems (WAMS), covering detection algorithms, communication reliability, and resilient control frameworks for modern power grids.

Advisor: Prof. Jesse Thornburg Team: Chisom Ogbogu (MSECE-AD)
