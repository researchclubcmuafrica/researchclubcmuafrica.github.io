Improving SSVEP BCI Spellers With Data Augmentation and Language Models

The research improved SSVEP-based brain-computer interface (BCI) spellers by using data augmentation and language models to boost accuracy and reliability, enabling more intuitive communication even when brain signals are unclear.

Deep Learning Published

Deep Learning-Based Lesion Segmentation for Early Liver Tumor Detection

The research developed a deep learning model combining ResNet34 and U-Net to improve liver lesion detection and segmentation, aiming to enhance early liver tumor diagnosis and treatment in Rwanda through scalable AI-driven healthcare solutions.

Advisor: Prof. Ahmed Biyabani Team: Diane Mugunga (MSIT)
