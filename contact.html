Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our research club.

How can I join the research club?

You can join by filling out our membership form on the "Join Us" page. We welcome researchers, students, and academics from all disciplines.

What types of research do you support?

We support interdisciplinary research across various fields including AI, climate science, healthcare, and more. Our focus is on collaborative research that creates meaningful impact.

Are there membership fees?

No, membership in the CMU Africa Research Club is completely free. We believe in making research collaboration accessible to everyone.

How often do you organize events?

We organize regular workshops, seminars, and networking events throughout the academic year. Check our events page for the latest schedule.
